Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a baked, round dough crust topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and a variety of toppings such as pepperoni, vegetables, sausage, or mushrooms. While it originated in Naples, Italy, pizza has become one of the most enjoyed foods around the world, with countless regional styles and flavor combinations.

Pizza is known for its crispy yet chewy crust, melted cheese, and savory toppings that can be customized to suit different tastes. It is commonly served as a family meal, at parties, or as a quick and satisfying dinner, making it a versatile dish enjoyed by people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • Garlic powder (optional)
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Fresh basil (optional)
